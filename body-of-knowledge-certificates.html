Accessibility Body of Knowledge

In 2011–2012, several people in the accessibility community called for the creation of an Accessibility Body of Knowledge inspired by the Usability Body of Knowledge and the Project Management Body of Knowledge, but the websites that were created to realise this idea seem to have disappeared. For example, the EU-funded eAccess+ network created the eAccess+ Hub, which contained content that was made available under the terms of a Creative Commons licence. In early 2018, the network's website has disappeared. A11y Buzz (on the domain a11ybuzz.com) has also disappeared.

  • Nourry, Olivier: How we could build a Body of Knowledge for Web Accessibility, Accessiblog.fr, 12.03.2012.
