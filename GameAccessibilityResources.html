Examples of Accessible Games

  • AudioQuake and Level Description Language.
    This is an open-source project to create an first-person shooter (FPS) that is accessible to blind and vision-impaired gamers.
    To download the game, go to Jedi Quake (a mod of AudioQuake).
    For more background, visit The AGRIP Project.
  • Terraformers – Accessible 3D game for blind & sighted.
  • UA-Chess: UA-Chess is a fully-functional chess game, developed by the Human-Computer Interaction Laboratory of ICS-FORTH in close cooperation with the Centre for Universal access & Assistive Technologies, in the context of the Universally Accessible Games (UA-Games) Activity.
    UA-Chess was developed using Macromedia Flash MX Professional 7. Speech recognition and synthesis is supported through the use of Speech Application Language Tags (SALT) technology.
  • Indian company launches talking chess board and app for blind people, Global Accessibility News, 15 May 2012.
